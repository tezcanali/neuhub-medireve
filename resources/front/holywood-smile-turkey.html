Who Is Suitable for Hollywood Smile ?

In fact, there is no need for a procedure like implants to get a Hollywood Smile Turkey. What Is The Operation Process of Hollywood Smile ?
When explaining the Hollywood Smile procedure, we can break it down into three main stages.
Step 1

After assessing the surrounding areas of your teeth and the gum tissues, detailed photos are taken, and 3D measurements of your teeth are taken. After these measurements and photographs, a design is created in a virtual environment, determining the size, shape, and color of your teeth.

Step 2

During this phase, other procedures such as teeth whitening, dental veneers, Emax crowns and crowns may also be applied. Then, the prepared teeth are examined to check for any imperfections. If any issues are found, the shape and size of the teeth are adjusted, misalignments are corrected, and any problems are addressed.

Step 3

In this step, the teeth that have been corrected are placed on the patient's teeth in a permanent manner. During the placement process, materials compatible with teeth, such as porcelain or zirconium, are used. Afterward, the color and shape details of the teeth are checked. Once they are placed, any additional procedures, if necessary, are performed to ensure a natural appearance.

The recovery time for a Hollywood Smile actually depends on how well you take care of your teeth. It's not like the recovery period after an implant procedure. However, the brightness and whiteness of your teeth will change based on the care you provide. For this reason, it is important to go for regular check-ups and maintain proper oral hygiene. You should brush your teeth at least twice a day and use dental floss. If food particles remain between your teeth, it could lead to inflammation.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

The number of grafts depends on the extent of your hair loss, desired density and donor hair quality. Most procedures require around 1.500 to 4.000 grafts. The important part is the growth rate of the transplanted grafts rather than the number.

Initial hair growth after hair transplantation starts around 3-4 months. Final results with denser hair can be seen around 12. months. Following the instructions during this period is important for good results.

The decision of shaving for a hair transplant depend on the size of the area, your hair type and preferences and the technique. FUE generally requires shaving for easier access and precision whereas in DHI, the patient may not need to shave the whole head. The exact information can be provided by the medical professional by assessing your case.
